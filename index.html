My Music Replay 2023
Curated by Linghao Shi -

As the world danced to the pulsating beats of 2023, the music scene witnessed an eclectic symphony of new trends and viral sensations. Chart-toppers were painted with bold strokes of experimental sounds, with artists pushing the boundaries of creativity, from the electric buzz of K-pop's global takeover to the resurgence of vinyl in a digital age. Yet, amidst this modern mosaic of melodies, my playlist remained a canvas of the past, a personal tribute to the timeless tunes that echo with memories. It's a year where the new waltzed with the old, and here on my blog, I've curated a collection that defies time – a harmonious blend where classic beats from yesteryears still reign supreme. Join me as I rewind through the tracks that defined not just the year but the essence of my musical identity.
